The American Hotel

  • Location and Time:

    The ceremony will begin at 5:30 PM at The American Hotel.
    125 S Augusta St #4100, Staunton, VA 24401
    Both the ceremony and reception will be held inside at the same location.

  • Parking:

    There is a two level parking garage next to The American Hotel that can be used all night. Cars can even stay the night and be picked up the next day if necessary. The top level is accessible from the side of the The American Hotel that faces the train station, so that level is preferrable for those who want to avoid stairs. The bottom level is accessible from Mill Street, and there is a set of stairs that allows access to the front entrance of The American Hotel.

  • Wheelchair Accessibility:

    The venue is wheelchair accessible. The front door of the venue is easily accessible and a great location for a drop off. There is an elevator that can be used in the building to reach the ceremony and reception hall.

  • Food:

    A buffet style dinner will be served with a variety of options. We will have gluten free and shellfish free options available. When sending your RSVP, please let us know if you have any dietary restrictions.

  • There will be a dance floor, and we encourage everyone to enjoy themselves!
  • Any questions or concerns? Please let us know info@wilsonwedding.info
